About Bagan

Bagan is home to more than two thousand pagodas and temples scattered across the plains along the Ayeyarwady River. It is the spiritual heartland of Myanmar and a window into classical Burmese architecture.

Arts & Crafts

Discover lacquerware artisans, mural restoration, and traditional wood carving that continue age-old techniques passed down through generations.

Iconic Experiences

  • Sunrise viewpoint over the temple plains
  • Temple cycling routes and guided heritage walks
  • Local markets and riverside villages

Location

Bagan, Mandalay Region, Myanmar

Best Time to Visit

November to February for cool, clear mornings.

Getting There

Flights from Yangon and Mandalay, bus routes, and river cruises.
